About Me

My name is Yasin Ghasemi. I'm an engineer with a vast fondness toward creating, building, and using tech in whatever shape or form possible.

I have an M.Sc. in mining engineering. As a mining engineer, you learn a thing or two about all aspects of engineering, at least it was like this in my school, but having a good knowlege of programming is not something you're expected from. My master's thesis introduced me to the world of programming. Right now, that thesis lives on my github. I called the project MinexPy, and maybe one day I'd write about all ambitions I had about it.

Fast forward to April 2026, a war between Iran and U.S.-Israel broke out. I was living in Iran back then. The Iranian government cut off international internet access. We couldn't use any international service for a very long while. I was utterly bored. I could only watch movies and read books. Then, an idea came to my mind. What if I designed my website like a Windows 98 desktop? Windows 98 was my first contact point with computer technology back in 2000 or 2001.

I used the little internet access I had to get to an AI coding assistant to help write this blog. Thus, this all came live in May 2026. I decided to keep it old school and static. So, I could easily publish it on GitHub using GitHub Pages. I'm not a big fan of Microsoft these days, but thanks to them, I have this website up and running.

Please read, interact, and explore as you wish, and let me know of your thoughts. Can't wait to hear from you!
